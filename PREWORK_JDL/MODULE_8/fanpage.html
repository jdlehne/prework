Who is Devo?

Devo sprung out of Akron Ohio in the early 70's when two friends's formed a satirical music duo at Kent State University. They chose the name "Devo" a tongue in cheek reference to the idea of humanity's "De-evoultion", the idea of the dumbing down of then modern civilization.

Overall the band would see some14 members drop in, out, pass away to form what was once thought to be a "Joke Band" but in 1978 recieved it's first record contract at the urging of artists like David Bowie and Iggy Pop. They're first studio album "Q: Are We Not Men A: We are Devo!" was produced by Brian Eno who also worked with Bowie, alng with many other bands including the Talking Heads, U2, Genesis and many more.

With a career over forty plus years, they would see 14 members come and go and release 9 studio albums in addition ot playing thousands of shows, numerous television appearances and many spin-offs and music production jobs in modern television shows and major motion pictures.

Main Members

  • Bob Casale - guitar, keyboards, backing vocals (died 2014)
  • Gerald Casale - bass guitar, vocals, bass synthesizer
  • Mark Mothersbaugh - vocals, keyboards, guitar
  • Bob Mothersbaugh -guitar, vocals
  • Alan Meyers - drums (died 2013)
